This project aims to study, analyze, and forecast the supply and demand of electrical energy for the City of Chicago from 2020 to 2027. It uses a combination of OpenAI GenAI models (gpt-4o-mini), AutoGen, and two agent types (CodingAssistantAgent and CodeExecutorAgent) to automate tasks in data engineering, analysis, and forecasting.
